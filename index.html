Burabai
The Burabay National Park is a natural park located in the Burabay District, in Aqmola Region, Kazakhstan, near Nur-Sultan. The park was selected as one of the top 10 tourist destinations in Kazakhstan. The park is under the direct jurisdiction of the President of Kazakhstan.

Charyn Canyon
Charyn Canyon is a canyon on the Sharyn River in Kazakhstan. The canyon is roughly 154 kilometres in length. It is part of the Charyn National Park, and is located within the territory of the Uygur District, Raiymbek District and Enbekshikazakh District.
